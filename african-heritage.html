Abstract in English

In African societies, culture plays a pivotal role in shaping our identities and fostering relationships among people. In this video presentation, we explore the significance of African culture and how it contributes to building sustainable communities. We will also highlight the importance of gender awareness in African society, and how gender systems impact people's lives on a daily basis.
